Instructions

Add rectangles, arrows, and text to the canvas image

To Add Text

Write your text, check text, and click on the image where you wish to add the text

To Add An Arrow

Choose which direction you wish your arrow to point, then click on the image where you wish to add the text

To Add A Rectangle

Select the rectangle option then click on the image where you wish to add the shape

You are also able to add a rectangle with text inside by entering your text and having both rectange and text selected before clikcing on the image
